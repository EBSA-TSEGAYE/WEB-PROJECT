About Us

SOS Hermann Gmeiner School

SOS Hermann Gmeiner School Jimma is an education project of SOS Children's Village. Which is a non-governmental organization that provides care and support to children who have lost parental care or are at risk of losing it. It was started over a decade ago with a kindergarten program, which is now attended by approximately 240 young children from SOS families and the community. After completing kindergarten, students have the opportunity to continue their education at the nearby primary school constructed by SOS Children's Villages in Jimma. In 2016, the school was upgraded from a junior school to a high school, providing quality education to around 1,600 students annually. Over the years, the school has not only served the public by providing quality education but has also played a crucial role in shaping responsible and morally upright citizens for the country. Its impact has been felt not only by the students but also by the wider community, benefiting from the school's commitment to excellence.

Vision

The leadership community of SOS HGS believes that quality education is the greatest means to alleviate our society’s chronic poverty. Without education, multigenerational poverty will continue. In accordance with their principle of "Poverty reduction begins in the minds of people." Hundreds of students receive financial aid from this school to continue their education. This program enables many needy students to finish school successfully and join universities thus elevating their families’ standard of living and wellbeing as well.
