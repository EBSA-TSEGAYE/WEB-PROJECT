Welcome To SOS Hermann Gmeiner School Jimma

SOS Hermann Gmeiner School Jimma is one of the Education Projects of SOS Children’s Village International in Ethiopia. it is committed to providing a caring and empowering atmosphere for every individual to reach their maximum potential. Our core values center on respecting and valuing all individuals, regardless of their faith or background. The school serves students from both SOS Children’s Village and the local community. SOS HGS is renowned for providing exceptionally high-quality education, as evidenced by the high percentage of students who are accepted into top universities locally and internationally every year. The school's staff and students, representing a diversity of faiths, share and uphold fundamental human values and beliefs while holding the dignity of each human person in high esteem.
